School Classes

Our school variety of fun and interactive classes designed to match every child's age and learning stage. Through engaging activities, creative play, and guided lessons, children develop essential academic and social skills in a joyful and supportive environtment.
KG A
KG A class is designed to develop children’s basic skills in reading, writing, counting, and social interaction through fun and educational activities. Students are encouraged to learn joyfully, fostering curiosity, confidence, and independence.
KG B
KG B class prepares children for elementary school by focusing on academic readiness, discipline, and responsibility. Learning is conducted interactively through small projects, group activities, and experiential learning that promote teamwork and critical thinking.
PG A
PG A class focuses on stimulating children’s sensory and motor development. Through play, music, and exploration, students begin to understand the world around them while enhancing communication and social skills in a warm and nurturing environment
PG B
PG B class helps children become more independent and confident. Through enjoyable and structured activities, students develop coordination, basic thinking skills, and positive habits to prepare them for the next stage of learning.

After School Classes

Our after-school classes offer children the opportunity to explore their interests and talents beyond regular lessons. These programs help students develop creativity, confidence in a fun and supportive environment.
