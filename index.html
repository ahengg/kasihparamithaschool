About Kasih Paramitha School

Kasih Paramitha School was established under Vidya Parahita foundation in 2016. It is located at Bhayangkara Street RT. 46 No. 03, Kr. Anyar, West Tarakan, North Kalimantan. It focuses on early childhood education.

Kasih Paramitha School approach emphasizes on curiosity, creativity and hands-on learning. Our teaching material is time-tested, unique, and crafted by our own teaching material director. This material is theme-based and draws from multiple early childhood models such Montessory and Fun based learning Methodology. We believe this blend offers children the well-rounded experiences and skill set needed for their future academic success.

We use English as primary language of instruction with enrichment in Chinese Mandarin and Bahasa Indonesia
